About the Department
At a Glance

Established: 1995.
The Department provides an excellent educational environment that aims at bringing out the best. Our curriculum encompasses the recommendation of IEEE and ACM joint committee on CS Curriculum, National Curriculum Revision Committee, and duly approved by the HEC, MoE and MoST.d

Why study with US?
What to Expect?
